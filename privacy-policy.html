Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Monster Block Blast (the “App”) is provided by uysal.okan. This page describes how the App handles data and privacy.

Data we process

The App may process certain data automatically to improve gameplay, diagnose crashes, and display ads. Details are provided below.

Data stored on your device

Game progress (e.g., high score, gold, power-up counts) and preferences (e.g., sound/vibration settings) are stored locally on your device. This data is removed when you uninstall the App.

Third-party services

The App uses the following Google services:

  • Google AdMob: To display ads in the App and measure ad performance.
  • Firebase Analytics: To understand App usage (e.g., sessions and basic interactions) and improve the experience using aggregated analytics.
  • Firebase Crashlytics: To diagnose crashes and fix errors.
  • Google Play Games Services: For leaderboards and achievements.

Advertising ID (Android)

On Android devices, the device advertising identifier (AD_ID) may be used for AdMob ads. Depending on your device settings and Android version, the advertising ID can be reset or restricted.

Data shared

For advertising, analytics, and crash diagnostics, the App may share certain technical data with the services listed above. This may include device information (model/OS version), App version, approximate location signals (such as country/language), crash logs, and usage statistics.

Children’s privacy

The App is not intended to knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe that personal information from a child has been collected inadvertently, please contact us using the information below.

Your choices

  • Ad personalization: You can manage ad personalization in your Google account settings.
  • Uninstall: Uninstalling the App removes locally stored game data from your device.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page.
